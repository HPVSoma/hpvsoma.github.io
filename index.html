HPV

Understanding the role of an informational video on knowledge, attitude and beliefs regarding Human Papillomavirus (HPV) vaccine within different communities served by NYU Family Health Centers

The Project.

This is a quality improvement study on HPV and HPV vaccine amongst the Sunset Park, Park Slope, and Flatbush communities of New York. We want to investigate the patients’ beliefs, knowledge, and attitude regarding HPV and HPV vaccine. We hope that learning more about these factors will allow us to increase vaccination rates and address parent’s concerns regarding the virus and the vaccine adequately.

Furthermore, we want to quantitatively assess how an informational video on the HPV vaccination may influence the participant’s understanding of disease associated with HPV infection and preventative aspects of the vaccine.

The project will take place in four NYU Langone Family Health Centers in Brooklyn, New York: Sunset Park Family Health Center, Park Ridge Family Health Center, Flatbush Family Health Center, and Seventh Avenue Family Health Center.
